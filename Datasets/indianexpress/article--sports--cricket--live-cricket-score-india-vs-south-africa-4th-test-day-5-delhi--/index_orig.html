    Day 4 recap

    When it rains it pours for two-ton Ajinkya Rahane

    Live Cricket Score, India vs South Africa: Ajinkya Rahane made 227 runs over two innings in the fourth and final Test.

    BATTING ON 99, Ajinkya Rahane would probably not have believed his luck with what was dished out to him by Imran Tahir. It was an innocuous delivery on leg stump, which India’s No.6 calmly flicked to square leg for a single.

    R Ashwin – master craftsman in form of his life

    Live Cricket Score, India vs South Africa: R Ashwin was at his best during the spell of 2/29 on Day 4.

    The Kotla crowd rose to their feet to welcome AB de Villiers, who was coming to play his final innings of the series. Moments before, they had been celebrating Temba Bavuma’s dismissal but AB’s arrival shifted attention. A fascinating contest was about to unfold – world’s best batsman versus the No. 1 off-spinner. And Ravi Ashwin was making his bowling feel like music with the lightness of his fingers.

    Sunil Gavaskar all praise for Ajinkya Rahane, feels sorry for Virat Kohli 

    Live Cricket Score, India vs South Africa: Virat Kohli fell 12 short of hundred at the Feroz Shah Kotla in New Delhi on Sunday.

    Former captain Sunil Gavaskar described Ajinkya Rahane, who scored a century in each innings of the fourth Test against South Africa here, as the most complete Indian batsman in the current team as he has “done well in all conditions”.
