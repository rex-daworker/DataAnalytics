Shah Rukh Khan, ‘Dilwale’ team play host to Sania Mirza

Shah Rukh Khan shared pictures of Sania Mirza's visit on Twitter and added she brought biryani for 'Dilwale' team.

By: PTI | Hyderabad | Updated: October 14, 2015 11:42 am

Superstar Shah Rukh Khan , Kajol, Varun Dhawan and the whole crew of “Dilwale” enjoyed a nice evening with tennis star Sania Mirza on the film’s sets here.

Shah Rukh shared pictures from the star’s visit on Twitter and added she brought biryani for the team.

The actor, 49, posted a picture of himself with Sania, Kajol, director-choreographer friend Farah Khan, Varun and actor Varun Sharma.

“@MirzaSania your returns are impeccable but the Biryani you ‘serve’ is untouchable. Your smile won over the whole set. Thanks,” tweeted the “Chennai Express” star.

@MirzaSania ur returns r impeccable but the Biryani you ‘serve’is untouchable. Ur smile won over the whole set. Thx. pic.twitter.com/hafPG2lURU

— Shah Rukh Khan (@iamsrk) October 13, 2015

“Awee… Anytime for you all.. Glad you finally got sometime to eat some biryani after that all nighter.. See u soon,” Sania replied to Shah Rukh.

Awee …Anytime for you all.. Glad you finally got sometime to eat some biryani after that all nighter.. See u soon😘 http://t.co/bC1KnfHh5N

— Sania Mirza (@MirzaSania) October 13, 2015

In an earlier tweet, the 28-year-old sportsperson thanked the “Dilwale” cast for making her laugh.

“Wonderful ‘Dilwale’ hosted me on set in my own city. Thank you for all the laughs @Varun_dvn @iamsrk @TheFarahKhan.”

Wonderful ‘Dilwale’ hosted me on set in my own city 😘 Thank you for all the laughs @Varun_dvn @iamsrk @TheFarahKhan pic.twitter.com/no59sUaI80

— Sania Mirza (@MirzaSania) October 13, 2015

Thanking her for the biryani, Varun wrote, “Thank you for the biryani and now u enjoy my city,” to which Sani replied, “Yes yes… And thank you for the green tea. Don’t know what I would have done without it…”

Thank u for the biryani and now u enjoy my city http://t.co/qQXVwSuSN3

— Varun Dhawan (@Varun_dvn) October 13, 2015

Yes yes.. And thank you for the green tea :p don’t know what I would hav done without it .. http://t.co/tsCgaP9TIL

— Sania Mirza (@MirzaSania) October 13, 2015

“Dilwale”, directed by Rohit Shetty, is set for a December 18 release.
