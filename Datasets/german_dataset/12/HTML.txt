<p style="font-weight:bold;"><br/>Dear visitor</p>
<p style="margin-top:-10px;">Welcome to Toms Deutschseite. I wrote this private homepage in 2005/2006
to teach my foreign girlfriend (today my wife) <a href="inhalt.html#grammatik" style="font-weight:bold;color:#FB9D00;">German grammar</a> as well as possible over the internet. 
For this purpose I prepared a lot of theoretical explanations and exercises based on books and other internet sources. 
In addition, you'll find <a href="inhalt.html#vokabeln" style="font-weight:bold;color:#FB9D00;">vocabulary</a> and <a href="inhalt.html#working_with_texts" style="font-weight:bold;color:#FB9D00;">text exercises</a>, hints for <a href="inhalt.html#aussprache" style="font-weight:bold;color:#FB9D00;">pronunciation</a>, tips for <a href="inhalt.html#rechtschreibung" style="font-weight:bold;color:#FB9D00;">German spelling</a> and further <a href="links/links.html" style="font-weight:bold;color:#FB9D00;">links</a> which deal with the German language.<br/><br/>
Almost all topics are written in English. All explanations and most of the exercises are available as printer friendly PDF-files.
<br/><br/>
We are happy to announce that the number of visitors has increased from month to month. In 2014 we had about 1500-200 unique visitors per day who loaded up to 2.500 pages (1GB - 2 GB traffic) per day.
<br/>
